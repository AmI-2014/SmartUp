Smart Butler

The Smart Butler project aims to create a system able to substitute a human butler in a domestic environment.

Why to choose it

The Smart Butler is..smart! It will be everywhere, ready to perform any task in the shortest time, but it will show up only when needed.
You will have an extra hand to manage your daily routine plus it will help you in many little things, relieving you from boring tasks and letting you enjoy your life with more energy!

How it works

Based on vocal commands and users’ agenda the Smart Butler will be able to recognize the activities that the user is performing or is going to thanks to a transparent sensors' network.

What it does

  • It will set properly the environment so that you can carry out your task in a comfortable way. You will never feel too hot or too cold neither the need to regulate room’s ligths
  • Nobody will bother you with Skype calls or chat messages in a crucial moment because the Smart Butler will set automatically all your social’s status on ‘busy’ mode
  • You will receive record of every person that called you or come to see you while you were busy and everyone will know when you will be avaiable again to have a nice chat with them!
  • You won’t never stain your recipes again while cooking because Smart Butler will read them out loud for you!
  • It will help you remember everything you planned for the day
